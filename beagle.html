Welcome to the Beagle

The Beagle is a small hound that is similar in appearance to the much larger Foxhound. It is a popular breed for hunting rabbits and other small game.

Trivia

  1. The Beagle is known for its excellent sense of smell and is often used in scent detection work.
  2. They are friendly and great with children.
  3. The Beagle's coat can come in a variety of colors, including tricolor and bicolor.
